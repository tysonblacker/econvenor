Does your group fit into one or more of these categories?

  •   The group operates to make a profit
  •   The group is a government agency, authority or department
  •   The group is, or is a part of, a major political party
  •   The group aims to spread or impose religion

A note for religious groups

We see a difference between groups whose purpose is to spread or impose religion and groups motivated to work against oppression by their religious belief.

If your group is motivated by religious belief to work against oppression, but doesn't seek to spread that belief to others, we would love you to use eConvenor.

Examples of religious groups welcome to use eConvenor include: a feminist Muslim women's group; a Jewish anti-war group; a Buddhist vegetarian food co-op and a Christian group advocating for refugee rights.
